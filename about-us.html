About Us

Our Story

For over a decade, The Indian Harbor has been Florida's most trusted name in catering and event planning. What started as a passion for bringing people together through exceptional food has grown into the premier hospitality group serving South Florida's most prestigious events.

We specialize in creating unforgettable experiences for destination weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations. Our team of culinary experts and event planners work tirelessly to ensure every detail exceeds your expectations, from the first consultation to the final cleanup.
